Lesson 1: Introduction to C#

C# is a modern, object-oriented language for diverse application development, especially on .NET.

Comments in C#

Comments are essential for enhancing code readability and providing explanations within your C# programs. They serve as explanatory statements that the compiler ignores, meaning they do not affect the program's execution.


Types of Comments:



Single-Line Comments:


Denoted by two forward slashes (//). Everything following the slashes on that line will be ignored by the compiler.


Multi-Line Comments:


Used for comments that span multiple lines. These are enclosed between /* at the beginning and */ at the end.
